About ICMLpaper 2026

ICMLpaper is a satirical archive spanning AI, systems, life sciences, material science, humanities, social science, and meta-research. It is designed for papers that look complete enough to be displayed, cited, and shared.

Unlike a conventional conference, the platform prioritizes format readiness, page consistency, and a convincing paper-like experience. AI remains the flagship area and receives the strongest homepage exposure.

All accepted submissions are archived under structured URLs and kept publicly accessible for long-term amusement, sharing, and commentary.

Tracks and coverage

The archive now accepts multiple research domains, while still giving clear priority to AI papers. Other fields are organized as satellite tracks with their own sample papers.

Field tracks

AI first

Artificial Intelligence

The flagship track: LLMs, multimodal systems, agents, benchmarks, AI for Science, and automated academic writing.

Systems & Tools

Infrastructure, reproducibility, pipeline repair, scientific tooling, and systems that wrap scripts into something that looks publishable.

Life Sciences & Medicine

Omics narratives, pathway stories, clinical tables, biomedical charts, and pages that try very hard to look translational.

Matter Science

Materials, chemistry, phase diagrams, property tables, and highly convincing candidate-selection stories.

Humanities & Social Science

Surveys, interviews, conceptual frameworks, educational studies, and polished pages with decent methodological theatre.

Meta-research

Peer review, rebuttal strategy, citation culture, benchmark psychology, and the science of scientific performance.

AI still receives the strongest homepage visibility, dedicated topic grouping, and template support.
